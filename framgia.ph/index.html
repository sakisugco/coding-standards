Our Service

We specialise in outsourcing solutions for development of software, web services, and smartphone apps. We mainly accept projects for dedicated service, but we also do contracting jobs for individual development project. The advantage of dedicated service is that we work with our client closely and look for solutions together to make products. Since we do a lot of agile development projects without specifications, advanced skills are required for our engineers. We have a lot of schemes in place to improve the skills of our engineers and we believe it is the best environment for engineers who want to improve their skills. Many of our clients are based in overseas offices and work together with our engineers, so communication is carried out smoothly and bugs and mistakes could be avoided.
